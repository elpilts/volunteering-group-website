About Us

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a nonprofit organization, nonrepresentative and nongovernmental, composed by european students from technical degrees. Our priority is to encourage the communication, cooperation and scientific and technological development between students from different european universities. We offer the students an additional formation to the one received at their universities, and support with the beginning of their professional careers.
